Junior Rider Development — The Role of Events

For junior riders, the right mix of competitive and educational events is essential to healthy development. Local schooling shows provide a low-pressure introduction to the show environment. Regional USEF-rated shows build competitive experience and qualifying points. Clinics with experienced trainers accelerate skill development between competitions. Youth community events build friendships and a sense of belonging in the sport. Elite Equine Marketplace's junior events section helps families find the full range of opportunities their young rider needs.

Pony Finals Qualifier Shows

For families with pony hunters, finding and planning around Pony Finals qualifying shows is one of the most important aspects of the annual show calendar. A pony must earn Champion or Reserve Champion at a USEF Premier or National rated show to qualify for Pony Finals — making the selection of qualifying shows a critical strategic decision for competitive pony programs. Elite Equine Marketplace lists Pony Finals qualifier shows to help families plan their qualification campaigns.
